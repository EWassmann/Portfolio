About Me

I'm a passionate robotics engineer currently pursuing my Master's in Mechanical Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University (GPA 3.96). With hands-on experience in SLAM, computer vision, and autonomous systems, I specialize in developing intelligent robotic solutions.
